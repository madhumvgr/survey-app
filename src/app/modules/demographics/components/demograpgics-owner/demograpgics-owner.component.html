Household Owner

During the questionnaire process you will be asked a set of 19 questions. You will be asked about your name, age, sex, marital status, languages spoken, education, occupation and certain behavioural questions (i.e., shopping behaviour).

Therefore, if you have all required information available for reference a front of you it should take you approximately from 10 to 15 minutes to complete.
